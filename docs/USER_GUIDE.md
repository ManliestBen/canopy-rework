# 🌳 Canopy — Family Guide

Welcome to Canopy, our family's wall calendar! This guide explains
everything the panel can do. Everything works by touch — just tap.

---

## Getting around

The **icon bar on the left edge** takes you everywhere:

| Icon | What it is |
|---|---|
| 📅 **Calendar** | Everyone's events — the main screen |
| 🧹 **Chores** | Your daily chore chart |
| ⭐ **Rewards** | Stars you've earned and what you can spend |
| ✅ **To-Dos** | The family to-do list |
| 🍽️ **Meals** | What's for dinner this week |
| 🛒 **Lists** | Shopping and other lists |
| 🖼️ **Photos** | Family photos and the slideshow |
| 🌙 **Sleep** | When the panel dims or shows photos at night |
| ⚙️ **Settings** | Grown-up stuff (may ask for the PIN) |

The **top bar** always shows the time, date, and today's weather.
The colored circles are us — one color per person.

---

## 📅 Calendar

### Reading it
- Each event is a colored pill. The color = whose calendar it's from,
  and a little face on the pill shows whose event it is.
- Long events (trips, camps) stretch across days as a banner.
- The red line in Day/Week view is "right now."
- Weather appears at the top of each day so you can plan.

### Changing views
Tap **Agenda / Day / Week / 2 Weeks / Month** at the top.
- **Agenda** is the "what's next" list — great for a quick look.
- Use **‹ Today ›** to move around; **Today** always brings you back.

### Just my events
Tap **your circle** at the top of the calendar to highlight only your
events. Tap it again to see everyone.

### Adding an event
Three ways:
1. Tap the **blue + button** (bottom-right corner).
2. In Day/Week view, tap an **empty time slot** — the time is filled in
   for you.
3. Tap a **day's name** at the top of a column.

Fill in what, when, where. Emoji in titles are encouraged: 🦷⚽🎂
Set **Repeats** for things like soccer practice (weekly) or birthdays
(yearly).

### Changing or deleting an event
Tap the event → **Edit**. Change anything and **Save**, or **Delete**.
- School/team calendars we subscribe to are **read-only** — you can look
  but not touch (change those on the school's website).
- If an event repeats in a fancy way Canopy's picker doesn't cover, it
  says "kept as-is" — your repeating rule is safe.

## 🧹 Chores

Everyone has a column with their chores for the day.

- **Did a chore? Tap the circle** next to it. ✓ Green means done!
- Your progress bar fills up as you finish.
- Each chore is worth **stars** (⭐) — they add up on the Rewards page.
- Tapped by mistake? Tap the circle again to undo.
- Use **‹ Today ›** to see other days.

Grown-ups: **+ Add chore** sets the name, icon, who, how often
(every day / weekdays / weekly), and how many stars it's worth.
Tap a chore's name to edit or delete it.

## ⭐ Rewards

Your card shows your **star balance**, this week's haul, and your
all-time total.

Ready to cash in? Tap **Spend stars**, enter how many and what for
("Movie night pick!", "Ice cream run"). Recent redemptions are listed
below so there are no arguments. 😄

---

## ✅ To-Dos

The family to-do list, sorted so the important stuff is on top:
**Overdue** (red!), **Today**, **Coming up**, **Someday**.

- Tap the circle to check something off.
- Tap **+** (bottom-right) to add one. You can give it a person, a due
  date, a category, and make it repeat (like "Water plants — weekly").
- Repeating to-dos come back on their schedule; checking one off only
  counts for today.
- Tap a to-do's words to edit or delete it.
- Tap a person's circle at the top to see just their to-dos.

---

## 🍽️ Meals

The week's menu. Tap any box (breakfast, lunch, dinner) to fill it in.

Planning tacos? Type the ingredients in the meal box (one per line) and
tap **Add to list** — they land straight on the shopping list. 🌮

---

## 🛒 Lists

- Switch lists with the buttons at the top (Groceries, Costco, …), or
  make a new one with **+ New list**.
- Type in the box and hit **Add** — that's it.
- The **suggestion chips** are things we buy a lot; one tap re-adds them.
- Tap the circle to check something off; **Clear completed** sweeps up.
- Tap the **little + circle** on an item to say who's picking it up.

Tip: the lists work great from a phone too (see "From your phone").

---

## 🖼️ Photos & slideshow

The Photos page shows our photo library. Tap **▶ Slideshow** to start
it right away, and pick which album feeds it with the folder menu.

When the slideshow is running, the panel still shows the time, weather,
and your next event in the corner. **Tap anywhere to come back.**

## 🌙 Sleep

On the Sleep page you can set:
- What the panel does at night: **stay on**, **go dark**, or **show
  photos** — and between which hours.
- Whether the slideshow starts by itself when nobody has touched the
  panel for a while.
- How long each photo stays up.

Asleep panel? **Tap it** — it wakes up for a few minutes.

---

## ⏱ Timer

Tap the **⏱ button** in the top bar. Pick 5/10/15/30 minutes or type
your own. Big countdown ring, chime at zero.
"Ten minutes until we leave!" has never been easier.

## 📣 Notes (announcements)

Tap the **📣 button** in the top bar to post a sticky note on the main
screen: "Dinner's ready!", "Dog walked ✓", "Gone to practice."

- Pick an emoji, who it's from, and how long it stays up.
- Anyone can take a note down by tapping its ✕.
- If email is set up, you can tick "Also email the family."

## 🌤️ Weather

The temperature in the top bar is tappable — it opens the full
forecast. A **red dot** on it means there's a weather alert; the alert
details are on the forecast page.

## ⚙️ Settings (grown-ups)

May ask for the family PIN if the lock is on.

- **Appearance** — pick a theme (Skylight is the classic look; there's
  also Dark, Bold, and Pride 🌈) and how "glassy" the panels look.
- **Household** — family name, weather location, device name.
- **Family members** — add people, pick their color and avatar. Colors
  show up everywhere: events, chores, lists.
- **Reminders** — how many minutes before an event the panel pops up a
  reminder (or turn them off).
- **Security** — set/change the family PIN, and lock Settings.
- **Daily email digest** — a morning email with today's events and
  to-dos.
- **Backup** — download a backup file of all settings and people, or
  restore from one.
- **Calendars** are managed from the Calendar page → **Manage**.

## 📱 From your phone

Canopy works in any browser on our Wi-Fi: go to the panel's address
(ask whoever set it up — something like `http://canopy.local:3000`).
Enter the **family PIN** and you're in — add groceries from the couch,
check the calendar from bed.

If it says remote access is off, the PIN hasn't been set yet
(Settings → Security on the panel).
